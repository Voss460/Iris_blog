New typewriter awaiting command!

Because I wished to attempt something rather old-fashioned, I asked Hurley to help me procure a typewriter from the Victorian age. It feels ever so different from the keys of a modern computer—how curious and delightful it is to tap upon! I cannot help but wonder what sort of adventures might appear upon the page if one were to compose the tales of a great detective upon such a marvellous machine. The very thought makes me quite giddy with anticipation!

Perhaps I ought to write a story in which a great detective of the 21st century, together with his faithful colleague, finds himself carried back to the splendid British Empire of the Victorian age—wandering through misty streets and narrow lanes paved with old stone, and there, in that former world so bright with glory, continuing to practise justice in his own noble way?

因为想尝试一些复古的操作，所以拜托福尔摩斯君帮我买来了维多利亚时期的打字机。手感和电脑键盘完全不一样，实在是太有意思了！如果用这样的机器编写大侦探的故事，会敲打出什么样的冒险呢？我真的好期待好期待呢！

嗯……要不要写一个让2000s的大侦探和他的助手回到维多利亚时代的日不落帝国，穿梭于迷雾和狭窄的石砖巷子里，在昔日的、光辉万丈的世界践行自己正义的故事呢？

Pray, Do Come and Say Hello to Waggy!

By a curious turn of fortune, we have begun keeping a cat from today onward! Waggy belongs to a dear friend of ours from Japan. The little creature is most well-behaved and sensible—and quite exceedingly adorable besides! Every morning it wakes me by gently nudging me with its soft, fluffy head, and after such a beginning it is quite impossible to feel cross for the rest of the day. No wonder our Japanese friend insists on keeping cats, even when he says he can scarcely afford his meals!

I have been wondering whether there might be some opportunity to write Waggy into a story. Perhaps it might become a clever assistant in one of the great detective’s cases! Though… if the great detective should happen to be allergic to cat fur, I fear matters might become rather troublesome indeed…

因为一些原因，我们今天开始养猫了！吾辈是我们一位日本朋友的猫咪。它很听话、很有礼貌，而且非常非常可爱！每天早晨被它用毛茸茸的脑袋蹭醒之后，一整天就都不会有坏心情了。难怪我们的日本朋友就算自己要吃不起饭了，也要养猫咪呢！

我正在思考把吾辈写进故事里的事情。要不要让它成为某起案件中，大侦探的聪明助手呢？不过，如果那个名侦探正好猫毛过敏的话，事情恐怕会变得不太妙呢……
