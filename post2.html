New typewriter awaiting command!

Because I wished to attempt something rather old-fashioned, I asked Hurley to help me procure a typewriter from the Victorian age. It feels ever so different from the keys of a modern computer—how curious and delightful it is to tap upon! I cannot help but wonder what sort of adventures might appear upon the page if one were to compose the tales of a great detective upon such a marvellous machine. The very thought makes me quite giddy with anticipation!

Perhaps I ought to write a story in which a great detective of the 21st century, together with his faithful colleague, finds himself carried back to the splendid British Empire of the Victorian age—wandering through misty streets and narrow lanes paved with old stone, and there, in that former world so bright with glory, continuing to practise justice in his own noble way?

因为想尝试一些复古的操作，所以拜托福尔摩斯君帮我买来了维多利亚时期的打字机。手感和电脑键盘完全不一样，实在是太有意思了！如果用这样的机器编写大侦探的故事，会敲打出什么样的冒险呢？我真的好期待好期待呢！

嗯……要不要写一个让21世纪的大侦探和他的助手回到维多利亚时代的日不落帝国，穿梭于迷雾和狭窄的石砖巷子里，在昔日的、光辉万丈的世界践行自己正义的故事呢？
